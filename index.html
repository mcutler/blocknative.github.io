It's time to humanize the blockchain.


Blockchain has introduced brand new behavior patterns. Like any big change, new users experience a steep learning curve, peppered with moments of confusion and frustration — which reduce adoption and engagement.

If end users are going to be successful, we need to add a sense of familiarity to this unfamiliar space. We have the opportunity to guide them, assist them, and explain what’s happening – in terms everyone can understand.

Introducing Blocknative. User experience. Made easy.


Blocknative is building embeddable widgets that translate complex blockchain technology into clear, relatable, human terms on behalf of end users. Our components are designed to add clarity to every interaction and boosts confidence in every user, across every interaction

See It In Action   Join the Movement  



Build a better user experience. In minutes.


You will soon be able to embed our widget-based components into your distributed applications to help your users be successful.

By adding a simple code snippet, you soon can:

  • Quickly onboard users to supported browsers with installed, unlocked, and correctly configured wallets.
  • Ensure transactions complete as expected.
  • Intercept and redirect transactions that are likely to fail.
  • Alert users when they submit duplicate transactions.
  • Set customizable parameters to make the experience feel native to your application.
  • Access audit metrics to understand wallet funnel performance.
